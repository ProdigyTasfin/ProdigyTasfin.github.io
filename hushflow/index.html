HushFlow features for background music and multitasking

An Android music player built for background listening, queue manager workflows, custom playlists where available, and sound customization.

Background and screen-off playback

HushFlow can continue playing supported audio while you open another app, minimize the app, lock the screen, study, work or relax. It is a background audio player for multitasking, not a promise of compatibility with unsupported streaming services.

Playback controls

Use notification playback controls, lock-screen controls, an in-app mini player, play and pause controls, track navigation and repeat playback.

Queue and playlists

Add tracks to the queue, reorder queued tracks, remove tracks, repeat playback and use custom playlist support where available for simple queue management.

Sleep timer

Set a music sleep timer with a countdown, stop after the current track, and support sleeping, relaxation sessions or study sessions.

Audio discovery

Browse supported categories including Focus, Sleep, Mood, Pop and Jazz for focus music, sleep music and background listening.

Audio Lab

Shape sound with a 10-band equalizer, bass boost, virtualizer, loudness enhancer, audio presets and saved sound settings. Audio-effect availability and performance may vary depending on the Android device, operating system and connected audio output.

Free and Premium

Free includes core background playback, selected ad-supported access and basic listening tools. Premium can include ad-free listening, custom playlist support, full Audio Lab access, equalizer controls, saved sound preferences and additional premium features. A Lifetime option may be available.

HushFlow FAQ

Can HushFlow play while the screen is off?

Yes. HushFlow is designed for screen-off playback of supported audio.

Can I use HushFlow while using other apps?

Yes. You can play music in background while using other apps.

Does HushFlow include a sleep timer?

Yes. The sleep timer can use a countdown and may stop after the current track.

Can I control playback from the lock screen?

Yes. HushFlow supports lock-screen controls and notification controls where Android allows them.

Does HushFlow include an equalizer?

Yes. HushFlow includes a 10-band equalizer and related Audio Lab controls.

Is HushFlow free?

HushFlow offers a free experience with core playback and selected ad-supported access.

What is included with HushFlow Premium?

Premium can include ad-free listening, custom playlist support, full Audio Lab access, saved sound preferences and additional premium features.

Do audio effects work on every Android device?

Audio-effect availability and performance may vary depending on device, operating system and connected audio output.
